# Audio Module (`frontend/src/audio`)

Sound effects for block interactions (currently disabled).

## Current Status

Audio is disabled in the MVP. The constructor returns early and `playSound()` is a no-op.

## Future Restoration

To enable audio:
1. Remove early return from constructor
2. Initialize AudioListener and AudioLoader
3. Restore sound playback in `playSound()` method
